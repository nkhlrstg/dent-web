Why Choose Us?

Smile Dental Care & Implant Centre is a Meerut based dental health care which is located in the center of the city & easily approachable by all means of transport. The Center offers all form of routine dental treatments as well as advanced dental & dental related minor surgical procedures such as RCT, Scaling, Dental Bleaching, Conventional & Cosmetic Dentistry, Orthodontic treatment, Oral Surgeries, Dental Implants, Fixed &Removable dental prosthesis and Full mouth Rehabilitation by globally trained & super specialized dental team, all available under one roof at affordable prices.

Our Mission

It is a Dental Boutique where all comprehensive dental treatments are done with latest techniques using all modern, state of art machines & instrumentations.
As a Boutique Dental Care, we are passionate in designing beautiful & comfortable smiles of our patients in a warm & welcoming ambience with friendly staff & total transparency. In our commitment to provide unparalleled services with the highest standards of dental care we keep ourselves updated with continuing dental education, follow stringent sterilization methods and scrupulous hygiene protocol through highly trained & experiences staff with minimum waiting policy.

We at the Centre take special interest in promoting Dental-Tourism, as well as helping patients to guide about dental procedures that are covered under various Dental Insurance policies.
