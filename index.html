About Us

Smile Dental Care & Implant Centre is a Meerut based dental health care which is located in the center of the city & easily approachable by all means of transport.

Our Team

Dr. Anjeev Tyagi
Founder
Dr. Anjeev Tyagi
Founder

A Graduate from Kuvempu University is a Cosmetic Dental Surgeon & Implantologist in his private clinic at Meeru for more than 18 Years. He received PG Certified training in advance Implants from Life-Care in 2004. He is a Certified Clinical Master in Both delayed & Immediate Implantology for more than 12 years & is a Life-Member of Indian Society of Oral Implantologists (ISOI). A fellow of Academic General Excellence (FAGE) and Independent Clinical Practioner (FICP). He is also attached to continuing Education & Research Programme (CERP), U.S.A.

Dr. Neha Tyagi
Co-Founder
Dr. Neha Tyagi
Co-Founder

A Graduate from Rajeev Gandhi University of health Sciences, worked as a General Practitioner till 2007 and later obtained her M.D.S. in 2010. Since then she has been involved in Various Undergraduate teaching Programs. She became a fellow member of Academic General Excellence (FAGE) in the year 2010, and is attached to Academics for more than seven years and presently serving as an Assistant -Professor in the Dept. of Conservative & Endodontics at Kalka Dental College & Hospital, Meerut.
She has an excellence in all forms of Endodontics and is extensively involved in wide range of Cosmetic Dentistry procedures and Smile Makeovers. She is a life Member of Indian Endodontic Society (IES) as well as American Association of Endodontists (AAE).
